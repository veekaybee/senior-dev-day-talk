Basic rules of data¶


Data rule #1: Law of entropy¶

Natural processes have a preferred progress towards chaos

Goal: "I want to know how many customers I have."

Current environment:

  • Apache Web logs
  • POS credit card information

"Let's standardize everything, cross-reference weblogs with credit card info, and throw it into Hadoop"

Result:¶

Takeaway: Your project will get complicated enough without throwing tons of new tools in. Evaluate each tool carefully. Add one on at a time.

Data rule #2: Pareto Rule¶

People use 20% of their tools 80% of the time because people stick to what they know.

From the Data Science Salary Survey 2015

Data rule #3: Conway's Law¶

Organizations which design systems ... are constrained to produce designs which are copies of the communication structures of these organizations

  • Make data available
